About Our School

Radhakuchi JB School has been a beacon of quality education in the community since 1985. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where every child can discover their potential and develop into well-rounded individuals.

Our institution emphasizes holistic development through a balanced curriculum that combines academic excellence with character building, sports, and cultural activities. We believe in fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a love for lifelong learning.

From the Principal's Desk

Principal

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire."

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Radhakuchi JB School. As the Principal of this esteemed institution, I am proud to lead a team of dedicated educators who are committed to nurturing the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of every student.

Our school stands as a testament to the power of quality education and holistic development. We believe that every child is unique and possesses unlimited potential. Our role is to provide the right environment, guidance, and opportunities for them to discover and develop their talents.

In today's rapidly changing world, we prepare our students not just for examinations, but for life itself. We instill in them the values of integrity, compassion, and perseverance, alongside academic excellence.

I invite you to join us in this wonderful journey of learning and growth.

Warm regards,
Dr. Ramesh Kumar Sharma

SEBA Recognition

Radhakuchi JB School is officially recognized by the Secondary Education Board of Assam (SEBA) and follows the state curriculum guidelines for primary education.

Our institution holds official approval from the Assam State Education Department, ensuring compliance with all educational standards and regulations.
